## 6. Sales Process

### The Funnel

```
Outreach/DM → Book demo call → Show up → Live demo → Close → Onboard
```

### Demo Call Script (framework)

1. **Ask about their business** (2 min) — what do they do, how many leads/month
2. **Find the pain** (3 min) — how do they handle DMs now? How many do they miss? What does a missed lead cost them?
3. **Do the math with them** (2 min) — "So you miss ~3 leads/month, each worth $500. That's $1,500/mo in lost revenue."
4. **Show the product** (5 min) — live demo, show a real conversation
5. **Price anchor** (1 min) — "A human setter costs $1,000/mo and works 8 hours. This is $X/mo and works 24/7."
6. **Close** (2 min) — "Want to start this week? First month is [founding price]."

### Onboarding (must be fast — target: under 1 hour per customer)

1. Connect their Instagram/Facebook account
2. Set up their business info, services, pricing
3. Define appointment types and availability
4. Test with 3-5 sample conversations
5. Go live

**Automate this as much as possible. At 7 new customers/week, you can't spend 3 hours on each.**

## 8. Churn Prevention (Critical)

At 194 customers, even 5% monthly churn = 10 customers/month leaving. That means you need 10 new customers just to stay flat.

**How to keep churn low:**
- Weekly report to each customer: "Your bot handled X leads this week, booked Y appointments"
- Monthly check-in call with every customer (until you have too many, then automate)
- Fix every bot failure within 24 hours
- The confidence threshold system: bot escalates to human when unsure instead of fumbling
- Make switching painful (in a good way): the longer they use it, the smarter the bot gets for their specific business

**Target churn: under 3%/month**

## 9. Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Bot quality not good enough | Confidence threshold — escalate to human when unsure. Review every conversation for first 50 customers. |
| Competitor enters Israel | Move fast. Lock in customers with annual plans at a discount. Build relationships, not just software. |
| AI costs spike | Monitor per-customer costs closely. Optimize prompts. Set usage limits if needed. |
| Partner doesn't deliver | Vesting agreement. Clear milestones. Monthly check-ins on progress. |
| You burn out doing sales + support | Hire a part-time VA by customer 50. Partner takes CS by customer 80. |
| Low show-up rate on demos | Send WhatsApp reminder 1 hour before. Keep time between booking and demo under 48 hours. |
| Customers don't see value | The weekly report is key. If they SEE leads being handled, they stay. |
